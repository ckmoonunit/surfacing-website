What Is Surfacing?

Built for the gaps therapy can't fill

Episodes don't happen in the therapist's office. Surfacing captures what's happening in real time - so when you do sit down with your clinician, your data speaks for itself.

Condition-aware logging

Each entry adapts to your diagnosis. PTSD, BPD, GAD, autism, chronic pain: relevant fields, not generic checkboxes.

Insight dashboards

Built-in charts surface your triggers, skill effectiveness, and intensity trends over time.

Privacy by design

AES-256 encrypted, on-device only. No accounts, no cloud, no analytics. Nothing to breach.

DBT, CBT & ACT skill tracking

Log the skills you used and rate what worked. Real evidence to bring to every session.

Encrypted clinical export

Hand your clinician a password-protected HTML report. Opens offline, fully self-contained.

Fully customizable

Build custom conditions, skills, and charts. The app adapts to you, not the reverse.
