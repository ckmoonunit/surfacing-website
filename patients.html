🧍 For Patients & Individuals

Your episodes. Your patterns.
Your proof.

Most of us leave the therapist's office unable to recall the week clearly. Surfacing captures the real-time detail your clinician needs — privately, securely, and entirely on your phone.

The Problem

Memory is a terrible clinical tool.

When your therapist asks "how was your week?" you're reconstructing from a blur of fatigue, anxiety, and whatever happened this morning. Important episodes get lost. Patterns stay invisible.

The recall gap is real

Retrospective self-report is consistently less accurate than real-time tracking. EMA (ecological momentary assessment) is the gold standard — Surfacing brings it to your phone.

A Typical Session — Before Surfacing

Monday's panic attack

Triggered while driving. Lasted 20 minutes. You forgot by Thursday.

Wednesday: skill worked!

Used paced breathing during a meeting. Actually helped. No note taken.

Friday: sleep crash

3 hours of sleep, missed self-care, isolating. You remember it, but not the details.

Session day: "How was your week?"

"...okay, I guess. A little rough."

The Surfacing Way

Log it when it happens. Review it when it counts.

⚡

30-second logging

Open the app, tap your condition, rate your intensity, note what happened. Done. Your data is saved and encrypted before the moment passes.

🎯

Condition-specific fields

If you have GAD, you'll log worry topics. If you have BPD, you'll log trigger type and regret level. Every field is clinically meaningful for your diagnosis.

🧰

Skill effectiveness tracking

Log which DBT, CBT, or ACT skill you used and rate how well it worked 1–10. Over time you'll see which skills are actually moving the needle for you.

📈

Pattern dashboards

See your episode frequency over time, your most common triggers, your peak times of day, and your skill success rates — all visualized automatically.

📤

Share with your therapist

Export a password-protected file that opens in any browser. Works universally within their HIPAA-compliant ecosystem — no app, account, or additional software required on their end.

🔒

No one else can see it

Your data never touches a server. No one at Surfacing has access. No cloud backup. No account. It exists only on your device — and you control every export.

Your Privacy

What "private" actually means here

🚫

No account required

Surfacing has no login, no email, no username. There's nothing to breach because there's nothing stored outside your phone.

🔐

AES-256 database encryption

The SQLite database is encrypted at rest using AES-256, the same standard used by banks and government agencies.

📵

Fully offline

The app works with no internet connection. No data is ever transmitted anywhere. Not even anonymized usage data.

🗑️

Delete everything, instantly

One tap wipes every entry from your device. There's no backup to recover from, no server copy lurking somewhere.

🤝

You control every share

When you export to share with your clinician, you set the password. No one else — including us — can decrypt that file.
